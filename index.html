Our Story

Where Quality
Meets Craftsmanship

Located at DKE Sawmill in Rilpola, Badulla, Namunukula Timber has been the region's most trusted supplier of premium-grade wood. From sturdy hardwoods for structural builds to fine-grain softwoods for delicate furniture, every plank we supply is carefully selected, seasoned, and treated to meet the highest standards.
